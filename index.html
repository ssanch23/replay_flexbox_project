Back on the Big Screen

Located in Ponce City Market, Replay Pictures is an independent cinema specializing in theatrical re-releases of timeless favorites, cult classics, and hidden gems.

Now Screening

Trainspotting

Danny Boyle | 1996

Hilarious but harrowing, the film charts the disintegration of the friendship between Renton, Spud, Sick Boy, Tommy and Begbie as they proceed seemingly towards a psychotic, drug-fuelled self-destruction.

Starring: Ewan McGregor, Ewen Bremner, Jonny Lee Miller, Kevin McKidd, Robert Carlyle

Zodiac

David Fincher | 2007

Fear grips San Francisco as the Zodiac killer claims victim after victim and taunts the authorities with cryptic messages and cyphers. Investigators and reporters become obsessed with learning the killer's identity and bringing him to justice.

Starring: Jake Gyllenhaal, Mark Ruffalo, Anthony Edwards, Robert Downey Jr.

Showtimes:

Heathers

Michael Lehmann | 1989

Veronica is part of the most popular clique at her high school, but she disapproves of the other girls' cruel behavior. When she becomes fascinated with a rebel named J.D., he teaches her a more devious way to play social politics: by killing the popular kids.

Starring: Winona Ryder, Christian Slater, Shannen Doherty

Showtimes:

No Country for Old Men

Joel & Ethan Coen | 2007

Llewelyn Moss stumbles upon dead bodies, $2 million and a hoard of heroin in the desert, but a methodical killer comes looking for it, with the local sheriff hot on his trail. The roles of prey and predator blur as the violent pursuit of money and justice collide.

Starring: Javier Bardem, Tommy Lee Jones, Josh Brolin, Woody Harrelson

Showtimes:

Children of Men

Alfonso Cuarón | 2006

In a chaotic world in which humans can no longer procreate, a former activist agrees to help transport a miraculously pregnant woman to a sanctuary at sea, where her child’s birth may help scientists save the future of humankind.

Starring: Clive Owen, Clare-Hope Ashitey, Julianne Moore, Michael Caine

Showtimes:

Prisoners

Denis Villeneuve | 2013

Keller Dover is facing every parent’s worst nightmare. His young daughter, Anna, is missing, together with her friend, Joy, and as minutes turn to hours, panic sets in. The only lead is a dilapidated RV that had earlier been parked on their street.

Starring: Hugh Jackman, Jake Gyllenhaal, Paul Dano
